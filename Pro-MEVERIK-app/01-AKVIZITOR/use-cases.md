# Use Cases for AKVIZITOR Application

## 1. 3D Property Scanning for Presentations
AKVIZITOR provides the ability to capture detailed 3D scans of properties, allowing real estate professionals to create immersive presentations for clients.

## 2. Virtual Tours
With the 3D models generated through AKVIZITOR, agents can create virtual tours that allow potential buyers to explore properties from the comfort of their homes.

## 3. Measuring Floor Spaces
The application offers precise measurement tools that enable users to accurately assess floor spaces, helping in planning layouts and ensuring optimal use of space.

## 4. Property Portfolio Management
AKVIZITOR facilitates the management of property portfolios by providing comprehensive data and visuals for each property, aiding in strategic decision-making.

## 5. Client Presentations for Real Estate Professionals
Real estate agents can leverage the detailed insights and visuals generated by the AKVIZITOR application to deliver compelling client presentations that enhance engagement and understanding.
